Dial In Sounds

Brandtson

The sound is pure Brandtson...loud, tight and melodic. Dial In Sounds pushes the bands' combination of tone, vocal harmony and songwriting to its maximum potential. On this round, you'll hear ten beautifully structured songs teeming with lush, full, hypnotizing sound. The guitars carry Brandtson's signature aggression, blasting forth with a ruthless barrage of distortion.

  2. The Rookie Year
  3. With Friends Like You

  8. Anything And Everything
  9. Little Rounder
